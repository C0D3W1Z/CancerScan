Features

Our intuitive and user friendly dashboard interface makes all the features of CancerScan accessible to anyone, allowing us to have more of an impact.

Our machine learning model coupled with computer vision allows us to accurately identify the risk of skin cancer present in users from a image.

Our machine learning model uses input from users in the form of answers to certain questions to identify the risk of lung cancer in our users.

AI Powered Chatbot

Our personalized, AI and LLM powered chatbot answers questions and provides users with more information about their condition through GPT-4 and prompt chaining with Voiceflow.
