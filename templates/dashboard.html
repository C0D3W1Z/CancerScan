About

Skin Cancer Identification

By undertaking this identification process, you will be able to determine whether you have skin cancer or not. Make sure you have a good camera and position your skin well for the most accurate result. You'll also earn 5 points as a reward for your participation.

Lung Cancer Identification

By undertaking this identification process, you will be able to determine whether you have lung cancer or not. Make sure to answer all 15 questions with the option that most accuratly describes you. You'll also earn 5 points as a reward for your participation.

Mental Training

Through the utilization of mental training, you have the ability to stay mentally fit and maintain good health, regardless of whether you are dealing with cancer or not. This practice empowers you to prioritize your well-being. You'll also earn 1 point per mental training assignment as a reward for your work.

Physical Training

By engaging in regular physical training, you can enhance your physical fitness and overall health, irrespective of whether you are affected by cancer or not. Embracing this practice empowers you to prioritize your physical well-being. You'll also earn 1 point per mental training assignment as a reward for your work.
